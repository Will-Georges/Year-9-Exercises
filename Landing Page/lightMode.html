Mercedes

Mercedes have returned as the Silver Arrows. After two years of their black livery in support of BLM, they have returned to silver. It wouldn't be a Mercedes car launch without speculation. After the reveal of the new livery, Mercedes posted 3D renders on their social media platforms. The two liveries were basically different cars. The sidepods and front wing were just some of the things that were completely different. We will have to wait for the first F1 private testing session in Barcelona to see which of the two liveries that will actually be using.

Alpine

The disappointment was immense. After revealing a striking blue car last year, Alpine delivered what looked like a last minute design incorporating pink due to their new major sponsor BWT. Further to their reveal, they also showed off a full pink car which will be used for Bahrain, the opening race of the season, to show off their new major sponsor.

Ferrari

The leaks were real! The Ferrari livery was leaked earlier in the week and to the disappointment of fans, they were real. The Ferrari car reveal was set to be a good one due to its 75th anniversary special livery since the first production Ferrari car in 1947. Although the livery was leaked, fans were still thrilled due to the beautiful design of the car. The car was arguably one of the most unique due to its weird sidepod design. With massive sidepods having a big dip in the middle, it was 'memes galore' making fun of the sidepods, however, after day 1 of testing, Ferrari looked to have speed which could be a result of the sidepods.
