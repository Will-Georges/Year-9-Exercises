The Paypal Mafia

The "PayPal Mafia" is a group of ex-employees or founders of Paypal who went on to build other successful technology companies such as SpaceX, Tesla, LinkedIn, Yelp, Palantir, Yammer and the non-profit Kiva.

Six members (Peter Thiel, Elon Musk, Reid Hoffman, Luke Nosek, Ken Howery, and Keith Rabois) have become billionaires.
